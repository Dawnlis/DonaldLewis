Welcome to my site. I'm Donald.
I grew up in Youngstown, Ohio. In my childhood I loved to play any computer games I could get my hands on. Mario Kart, Sims 2, Tetris. I also spent way too much time watching cartoons! Fairly Odd Parents, Jimmy Neutron, Spongebob, Danny Phantom, Dragonball Z. I was obsessed with the creation of these things. How could someone draw on paper or type some code and create games and characters that really felt alive?
This hybrid experience of video games and cartoons is what I think gave me my passion for computers and animation. Animators now use computers more than ever to bring their creations to life so they can be experienced and loved by people across the planet. I think this relationship between computers and art is incredibly interesting and is what I strive to excel at in my career. Not just animation, but designing technology for the user and creating a UX worth using.
